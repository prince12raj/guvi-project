India International Institute of Democracy and Election Management

The Election Commission of India (ECI) established the India International Institute of Democracy and Election Management (IIIDEM) to advance its professional competence in election management.

The management of elections has become increasingly complex due to factors such as an increase in eligible voters and the rising number of political parties.
